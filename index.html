Featured Projects

Kakamega County Hand Well Borehole Project

Location: Kakamega County

SouthWest Drilling Company successfully completed a project for the Family Humanitarian Organization, drilling two hand well boreholes in Kakamega County. The project provided a reliable source of clean water for the local communities, significantly improving their health and well-being. SouthWest Drilling Company demonstrated exceptional professionalism, efficiency, and commitment to quality throughout the project, showcasing its expertise in drilling services and dedication to community development.

Kideket Borehole Water Project

Location: Kideket Village,Narok County

Southwest Drilling Company was selected to execute the Narok County Kideket Borehole Water Project.This important initiative, spearheaded by the Narok County Government, aimed to improve access to clean water for the residents of Kideket village.Our team of experienced drillers and technicians worked diligently to successfully complete the borehole drilling component of this project. Leveraging our expertise and state-of-the-art equipment, we were able to efficiently and effectively drill the borehole, ensuring a sustainable water source for the local community.

Lodwar Water-Well Drilling Project

Location: Lodwar, Turkana County

In the heart of Turkana County, known for its harsh, arid climate, Southwest Drilling Company undertook a critical water infrastructure project to establish a reliable water source in the town of Lodwar. Tasked by the Turkana County Government, our team of seasoned drilling experts navigated the technical complexities of the region's tough terrain, leveraging their extensive experience and state-of-the-art equipment to successfully complete the borehole drilling. Despite the notoriously dry conditions and high temperatures, our team persevered, driven by a steadfast commitment to providing the community with access to this vital resource. The successful completion of the Lodwar Borehole Project has had a transformative impact on the local population, alleviating the daily struggle of residents who previously had to travel long distances to fetch water. This project has not only improved access to clean water but has also contributed to the overall well-being and development of the community, showcasing Southwest Drilling Company's expertise and dedication to making a tangible difference in the lives of those living in the most arid and underserved regions.
